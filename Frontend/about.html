Our Journey

Nestled in the heart of breathtaking natural landscapes, Paradise Lost began as a hidden gem — a sanctuary where adventure meets serenity.

From zip-lining across canopies to bonfire nights under the stars, Paradise Lost is more than a place — it’s a feeling, a memory, a story waiting to be written by you.

Our Mission

Connect people with nature through immersive experiences.

Encourage environmental conservation and learning.

Create unforgettable memories rooted in adventure.

Our Vision

Be Africa’s most beloved nature sanctuary.

Transform lives through nature and peace.

Shape a future where nature is valued by all.
